## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 💳 TrustMetric — AI-Powered Credit Intelligence Platform
> *Redefining creditworthiness beyond traditional credit history*

A dynamic credit scoring engine that converts raw financial data into an intelligent trust score (300–900), empowering users who lack conventional credit trails.

**Key Highlights:**
- 📊 Multi-dimensional scoring across **5 financial pillars**:
  - 💧 Liquidity (25%) · 🎯 Discipline (25%) · 📈 Stability (20%) · 🛡️ Asset Protection (15%) · ⚠️ Risk Behavior (15%)
- 🧮 Normalized score mapping: **0–100 → 300–900 scale** with risk tier classification
- 🧠 Predictive insights for **creditworthiness & loan eligibility**
- 💡 Designed to make financial inclusion accessible to all
